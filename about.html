How We Started

My name is Nick Husband. I am a disabled veteran that is retired from the USCG. I served as a Damage Controlman and trained in many different skills throughout my career. I use these skills to create special one of a kind items. We are a brand new company looking to create custom gifts that are personalized just for you. I volunteer as a firefighter and an active animal advocate. I turned a hobby of mine into a business called Hubbys Creations, a perfect name for a guy with the last name Husband. I love to make special items for people and have been doing it for a long time. Everything is built with good quality products to make them last.
