How To Order

  • We are currently accepting orders via phone/text and email. Contact Us!
  • Preparing your order is the most important step process. Please review the information and contact us if you have any questions, prior to placing your order.
  • Get up to 2 free quotes per job. Quotes are valid for 2 weeks from the day you receive the quote by email or phone.
    • We will offer up to 2 proofs for free of charge. A $12.00 fee will be charged for each additional amendment.
    • Cancellations will not be accepted unless received in writing and confirmed by Hubbys Creations.
    • Charges will apply for any materials, shop materials, graphic charges, incidental charges and work completed to notification without exception.
    • Hubbys Creations reserves the right to refuse any engraving order for any reason without explanation.
  • Upon Request- Please fill out all information regarding the item(s) you wish to have quoted. Include detailed instructions for all orders to be done. Please provide any information necessary to explain exactly what you are looking for, more information is always better!
  • Orders will be completed upon full payment unless specified by Hubbys Creations.
